2022-03-08

全名《When the Pawn Hits the Conflicts He Thinks like a King What He Knows Throws the Blows When He Goes to the Fight And He’ll Win the Whole Thing ‘Fore He Enters the Ring There’s No Body to Batter When Your Mind Is Your Might So When You Go Solo, You Hold You》，来自Fiona Apple，由On the Bound开头，紧凑但不显腻的鼓点拉开整张专辑的序幕。

Fiona Apple的唱法让人更觉得这是一张爵士专辑，在第二首曲子To Your Love当中，诡秘旋律和毫不费力的怒音一起谱写了独立音乐女王的叛逆：“The shame is manifest in my resistance to your love”——与曲名给人的第一印象不同，这并非一首甜蜜情歌，而是距离感与爱情中的痛苦宣泄。
